Number 1: WRETCHED HANDS

This video was possibly based more on the classic found footage films, such as Blair Witch, with the message at the start about the live footage.

The text also states the message was around 25 minutes before 'first attack', followed by a radio station playing as the image zooms into a part of the background; this image turns to footage of a hiker travelling through the mountain. The video cuts to an old letter, as classical music plays and the contents of the letter is displayed through subtitles. The message involves a plan to deal with 'beings' without giving in to them, planning to offer a bear to 'The Statue'; the closing message to someone called Curtis hints towards a deal made with the beings. Another letter is shown, appearing to be as someone is being stalked by something. Imagery of a wooded area is shown, with body parts and peoples names being listed and outlines fading in over the areas. A final message states that an organism is mutating, originally a bear but it has now become unrecognisable, now resembling a tree-like shape of flesh.

Using aspects of body horror, especially towards the end, and being fully self-contained but with possibilites to expand on the story, this video is a great example is someone wants to get into the genre. .
